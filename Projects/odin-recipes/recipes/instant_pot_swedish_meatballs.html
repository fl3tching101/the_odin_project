Description

Simple Swedish meatball recipe using the instant pot

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef meatballs frozen or thawed
  • 2 1/2 cups low sodium beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 cups extra broad egg noodles (140g)
  • 1/2 cup half and half or heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon corn starch

Steps

  1. Place meatballs in the Instant Pot with beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, parsley, salt and pepper. Stir until spices are combined.
  2. Add noodles, spread into an even layer and press down gently but do not stir.
  3. Put the lid on, turn the valve to sealing, and select Manual or Pressure Cook for 3 minutes. It will take about 10 minutes for the Instant Pot to come to pressure.
  4. When the cook time is over, let the pressure release naturally for 5 minutes, then open the valve to release the pressure, and remove the lid.
  5. Whisk together cream and cornstarch and stir into meatballs. Turn the Instant Pot to saute and cook and stir just until sauce has thickened
