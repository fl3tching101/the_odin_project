Italian Sausage Tortellini

Back home

Description

A tasty dish combining cheese tortellini, spicy (or mild if you prefer) italian sausage, and marinara sauce as the main ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 20 Oz Package of tortellini (any variety, I like five cheese)
  • 16 Oz Spicy (or mild) Italian sausage
  • Half a jar of marinara sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 4 Oz Cream cheese (plain)
  • Frozen chopped spinach as desired

Steps

  1. Brown Italian sausage in a deep pan. Also put a pot of water on to boil.
  2. Once browned, add marinara, cream cheese, and chopped spinach.
  3. While the sauce simmers, cook tortellini in the boiling water as instructed on package.
  4. Once suace is fully combined and spinach is wilted, combine the cooked tortellini noodles and a splash of pasta water.
  5. Simmer to prefered consistency and serve.
