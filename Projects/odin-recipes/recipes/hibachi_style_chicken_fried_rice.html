Description

Chicken fried rice recipe inspired by a trip to the Hibachi grill

Ingredients

  • Chicken
    • 1 chicken breast, diced
    • 1 TBSP soy sauce
    • 1 TBSP butter
    • Â½ TSP sesame oil
  • Fried Rice
    • 1 c. uncooked white rice
    • Frozen peas and carrots, as desired
    • 2 eggs
    • 3 TBSP soy sauce
    • 1 TSP sesame oil

Steps

  1. Add uncooked rice and water to the instant pot, and cook on rice function (high pressure for 12 min). Once the rice cooks, allow natural release for 10 min.
  2. Heat a drizzle of vegetable oil in medium pan over medium heat. Add diced chicken and cook through. Once cooked, add soy sauce and butter. Once the butter melts, add sesame oil. Reduce heat to low and simmer.
  3. While the chicken cooks, cook the eggs, scrambled, in a large pan. Then, add the peas/carrots and a drizzle of vegetable oil. Once the vegetables cook, add the cooked rice. Incorporate soy sauce and sesame oil and fry over medium heat as desired.
